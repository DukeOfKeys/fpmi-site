Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions about our platform

How does the AI diagnostic work?

Our AI diagnostic uses advanced machine learning algorithms to analyze your current knowledge and skills. It evaluates your vocabulary, grammar, pronunciation, and listening comprehension, creating a complete map of your strengths and weaknesses.

Can I use the platform on mobile devices?

Yes, Edu AI is fully adapted for use on mobile devices. You can learn anytime, anywhere through our mobile app or the adapted mobile version of the website.

How often is my study plan updated?

Your study plan is constantly adapting based on your progress. Every completed task, every lesson passed affects the further learning trajectory. Major adjustments occur after passing key milestones or with a significant change in your goals.

Is there a free trial period?

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial period for all new users. During this time, you will get full access to all platform features to evaluate whether our learning approach is right for you.
